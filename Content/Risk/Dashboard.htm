Risk Management BI Dashboard

Using the Risk Management Dashboard and its associated reports, you can track Workers’ Compensation Claims by Incurred Value, Claim Specialist, Frequency, and much more. The dashboard provides visibility into Workers’ Compensation risk for your PEO.

The dashboard provides the following reports:

  • Incurred Value By Year
  • Inc Value by Cause
  • Claim Frequency By Year
  • Severity By Code
  • Top Ten Open Claims
  • Frequency By Code
  • Top Ten Clients By Incurred (since 2016)
  • Claim Listing By Client
  • Open Claims By Specialist
  • Risk / Claim List By Type / Chart
  • Frequency By Year

 

To access the dashboard:

  1. From the modules bar, select Business Intelligence.
    The list of Business Intelligence reports displays.
  2. Select Risk > Risk Management Dashboard.
    The dashboard displays.
