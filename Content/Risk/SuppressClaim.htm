Suppressing claims on OSHA Logs

If you do not want a particular claim to be included in the OSHA 300A report, select the Suppress on OSHA Log option.

To suppress a claim on OSHA Logs:

  1. On the modules bar, select WC Claims.
    The WC Claims dashboard opens.
  2. Select the claim in question and click (Open).
    The Comp Claim form opens.
  3. In Additional Claim Information, select the Suppress on OSHA Log option.

  1. Click Save.
