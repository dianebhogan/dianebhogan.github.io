Hiding or showing inactive Workers' Compensation policies

Hiding or showing Inactive Workers' Compensation Policies is a personal choice for your organization. From any dataform field detail that is configured as a Workflow Channel Policy datatype, add one of the following settings to the Related Fields field.

Valid Settings

IncludeInactive=true

IncludeInactive=false

Default Behavior

If left blank, then the field behaves as if it were set to IncludeInactive=true.
