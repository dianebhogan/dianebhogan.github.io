Finding your installed version

Want to know which version of your company is using?  Perform the following steps to find out.

To get the Core and PEO versions of your install:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. In the lower right, you see the version information.

  1. Compare this version information to the header of the release notes when reviewing release features and enhancements. 
    • R## represents the release number.
    • The following numbers are the release date stamp.

For all other release related questions, or to schedule an upgrade for your install, log an Extranet case.
