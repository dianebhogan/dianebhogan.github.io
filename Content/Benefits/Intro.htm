Concepts of Benefit Plan Manager

ClientSpace Benefit Plan Manager is a benefits administration system that:

  • Manages master and client level plans.
  • Generates benefits comparisons, quotes, and benefits election forms.
  • Manages benefits for general plan categories (health, vision, and dental).

 

What is your benefits administration model?

  • Agents
  • Quoting and underwriting
  • Claims advocacy
  • Change management process

 

What is your benefits plan structure?

  • Master plans
  • Client plans
  • Rate groups
  • Age banding
