Integrations

provides integration and APIs for the following applications:

  • DocuSign
  • PlanHub
  • Salesforce

DocuSign

DocuSign is one of the most widely used e-signature applications in the world allowing you to sign documents anywhere from any email-enabled device without overnighting, faxing, or waiting.

Read more about the DocuSign integration in the Setup & Administration Guide.

See DocuSign integration for how to configure ClientSpace to connect to DocuSign through the API and provide integrated electronic signatures for your ClientSpace documents.

PlanHub

PlanHub allows you to create instant, customized 401k cost comparison proposals for your clients and prospects, as well as to enroll your contacts into an automated email campaign to nurture them towards sales-readiness with the value of the 401k at the forefront. For more information about PlanHub, see their website.

With the PlanHub integration, you can request a Slavic 401k PlanHub proposal from within – offering you an integrated solution. From , you can create instant, customized 401k cost comparison proposals for your clients.

The PlanHub integration passes the prospect’s FEIN to Slavic401k, and if successful, a PDF version of the PlanHub proposal is received and uploaded to the Slavic PlanHub dataform. When the PlanHub proposal is received, it can trigger established workflows to notify individuals for any additional next steps.

Read more about the PlanHub integration in the Setup & Administration Guide. See PlanHub integration.

Salesforce

The Salesforce API Integration is a PEO process that maintains data synchronization between a client’s installation and their respective Salesforce system. 

During the life cycle of a Deal, either Salesforce or is responsible for the data. Typically, when in the Lead Discovery cycle, Salesforce owns the data. As changes are made in Salesforce, is updated with those changes. When the Deal has reached a certain level, will no longer accept changes from Salesforce but will keep Salesforce up to date with certain data as changes are made in . 

Read more about the Salesforce integration in the Setup & Administration Guide. See Salesforce integration.
