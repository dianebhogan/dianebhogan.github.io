Executive Dashboards

The new Dashboards provide real-time executive-level insight into client performance. The dashboards help you identify clients who need additional support, and also help you understand the financial impact of trends across your client base.

  • Book of business performance dashboard provides a series of trends across your entire client base, including:
    • Gross Wages and Admin Fee
    • Gross Profit

    • Number of Worksite Employees Paid (aggregate and by state)

    • Client Employee Details

  • Client performance dashboard provides an analysis of performance data and trends by clients to help you proactively offer additional support, including:
    • Admin Fee Trends
    • Gross Profit Trends
    • Revenue By Client
    • Worksite Employees Paid by Month
