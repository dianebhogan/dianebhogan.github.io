Part 2: Contact syncing on an ongoing basis

After the initial standardization of Contacts completes, you are ready to adopt the practice of entering contacts in either or PrismHR.

Do NOT enter contacts in both systems if your organization adopts the bi-directional sync functionality.

The import and export processes will dutifully do the following:

  • Import new contacts entered into PrismHR into ClientSpace and stamp the proper ImportID. This occurs if the HRPAPI_contact*.csv import mapping is active AND the ContactImportBehavior parameter is set to Insert or Both.
  • When the HRPExportContactsSync scheduled process is active: When a contact is saved and the Client Number is not empty, automatically enables (selects) the Sync with PrismHR option.
    • The HRPAPIExportContactsSync process identifies the records that have the Sync with PrismHR option selected, and exports them to PrismHR.
    • The Sync with PrismHR option is not selected when a contact is saved from an Import. It is only applicable to contacts who belong to a Workspace with status = Client.

  • The HRPAPIBiDirectionalContactsSync scheduled process should be disabled (off, not active). This scheduled process is ONLY used for the initial clean up effort.

  • The PrismHR Export Contacts scheduled process continues to process the contacts with Sync With PrismHR option enabled (selected) and enters those Contacts in PrismHR.
