PlanHub

PlanHub allows you to create instant, customized 401k cost comparison proposals for your clients and prospects, as well as to enroll your contacts into an automated email campaign to nurture them towards sales-readiness with the value of the 401k at the forefront. For more information about PlanHub, see their website.

Why do I care about this?

With the PlanHub integration, you can request a Slavic 401k PlanHub proposal from within – offering you an integrated solution. From , you can create instant, customized 401k cost comparison proposals for your clients.

What does it do?

The PlanHub integration passes the prospect’s FEIN to Slavic401k, and if successful, a PDF version of the PlanHub proposal is received and uploaded to the Slavic PlanHub dataform. When the PlanHub proposal is received, it can trigger established workflows to notify individuals for any additional next steps.
