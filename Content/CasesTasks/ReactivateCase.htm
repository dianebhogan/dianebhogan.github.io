Reactivating cases

You can reactivate a case after it has been closed.

To reactivate a case:

From the Cases list, click More.

The Standard Filters dialog box opens.

For Show, click Archived and then click Search.

The archived cases are displayed with italics and bold.

Locate the targeted case and click or (Jump) or Edit.

The Client Service Case form opens.

In the Action Center, under Workflow, click Reactivate.

The case is now reactivated.
