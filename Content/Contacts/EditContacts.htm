Editing contacts

After you add a contact, you can return to the Organization detail record and edit the contact record. For example, perhaps you want to send out birthday greetings to your contacts. You can update the Birthday field in the contact record.

To edit contacts:

  1. Open the Organization record.
  2. In the Action Center, expand Contacts.
  3. Select a contact.
    The Contact Detail form opens.
  4. Apply your changes and click Save.
