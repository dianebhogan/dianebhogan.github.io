Conditionally require a dataform by WC Codes

To conditionally require a Dataform for a group of WC Codes you just need to configure the HE_RequiredTablePerCodeEngine business rule.  In the following example, this is configured to fire on Submit for the codes 8810 and 8742.

Configuring this type of Hard Error will also put a link on the Header of the configured dataform to access the required Dataform and complete it. 

The Code List field should be a comma separated list of applicable codes with no spaces.
