Best practices: Sales workflow

Business development is the lifeblood of any business. A cohesive approach and system that manages the activity, presentation, and controls to convert leads to clients is critical to success in any business. A well-defined process and supporting system can yield dramatic improvements in results such as consistently increasing the new business pipeline, increasing the closing ratio, improving the quality of the book of business, and maintaining a strong positive first impression for new clients. integrates all the players and processes involved in converting leads to strong new clients.

Common terminology

Lead: a sales target that has not yet indicated any reasonable expectation of buying

Prospect: a sales target that has indicated potential intent to buy or is actively considering a purchase

Client: a company that has committed to purchase

Implementation: the unique set of business processes required to bring on a new client

Sales activity stages

Lead generation: identifying and collecting new sales targets

Lead nurturing: engaging with sales targets to determine fit for product or service

Closing the deal: data collection, quoting, proposal, approving, ending in client commitment

New client implementation: activities required to deliver the product or begin servicing a new client
