Enabling the Email to Case Discussion business rule

The business rule for Email to Case Discussions is already configured. However, you must activate the rule. This topic guides you through enabling the rule.

To enable the Email to Case Discussion rule:

  1. Go to System Admin > Advanced > Manage Rules.
    The Custom Logic Rules dashboard opens.
  2. Locate and open Discussion Message with Rule Title Send EmailIntegrationReply.
    The Rules Details form opens.
  3. Select the Active option.

  1. Click Save.
