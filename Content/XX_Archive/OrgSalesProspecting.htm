Think of an organization as an organizing folder or Rolodex card for storing everything about a potential client. The organization provides you with a single place to put lead related tasks and calendar events (client visits) as well as phone calls, emails, and follow ups.

Lets start by filling out some Demographic information on your prospective client - for the purposes of our example we are going to use the local software manufacturer Monkeysoft. When you first start approaching a prospective client you may only have some basic information, such as name address and phone number, but that is enough to get you started.
