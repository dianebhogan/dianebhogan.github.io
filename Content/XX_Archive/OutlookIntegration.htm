Integrating Outlook with

One of the best methods of integrating the Organization record in with the sales process is through the Outlook Add-In Post and Send functionality. The Outlook Add-In allows you to connect emails that you send and receive in MS Outlook to the contact and organization records that you use for Sales prospecting. When you have installed and configured the Outlook add-in for use, attaching emails to a Contact record in is only a click away. 

Simply create an email to your customer as you normally would in Outlook and when you are done, use the Post and Send function of the Outlook add-in to send your email and automatically attach the email to the related contact record in at the same time.

When you click Post and Send, the Outlook add-in uses the email addresses in the To and Cc fields of Outlook to look up contacts in your installation of and automatically tie the emails to the appropriate Contact record. 

Emails received from Client contacts can be attached to contact records as email activities in a similar way using the Post to ClientSpace function in Outlook. This button, available on received emails in Outlook, allows you to attach received emails to contact records, making these emails available from the Activities area of the Organization.
