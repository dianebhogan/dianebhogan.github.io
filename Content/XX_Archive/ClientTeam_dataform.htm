Configuring the Client Team dataform

In this step, you unhide the PHR Support Dashboard fieldset.

To configure the Client Team dataform:

  1. Go to System Admin > Advanced > Dataform Admin.
    The Dataform Admin dashboard opens.
  2. Open (click Jump) the Client Team dataform.
    The Dataform Properties form opens.
  3. In the Action Center, click Fields.
    The Client Team fields display.
  4. Locate and open (click Jump) PHR Support Dashboard.
    The PHR Support Dashboard form opens.
  5. In Settings, clear the Hidden option (if selected).
  6. Click Save.
