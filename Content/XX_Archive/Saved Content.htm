Some of the key areas of the Organization detail record are:

  1. Activities Icons: Enable the sales rep to quickly add Tasks, Emails, Notes, Phone call records, and Follow-up records that can act as a tickler file, prompting the salesperson to follow up with potential clients on a future date. 
  2. Generate Quote: After the sales process has moved to the point where the potential client has requested pricing, click the Generate Quote link to create a workspace using the data collected during the sales process and stored on the Org.
  3. Status: For tracking workflow through the sales process, beginning at Lead and ending at Client. These statuses enable sales management to track and understand their companies sales pipeline, and build sales forecast models. Status is configurable to meet the sales workflow of your company.
  4. Category: Tracks the type of organization the record pertains to and includes such options as Client, Broker, Partner, Vendor, and so on.
  5. EIN: Contains the Federal Employer ID of the potential client. If the EIN is not known, Applied can be used as a valid system value.
