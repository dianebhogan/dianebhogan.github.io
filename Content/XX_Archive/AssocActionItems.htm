Associated areas

Some of the other areas associated with tasks include:

Time Tracking: If Time tracking is enabled you can even track time for a given task using the time tracking buttons in the task header to get a better understanding of where your organization is spending its time.

Notifications: Select a list of users you want to keep in the loop about this task and they will be automatically emailed when the task is updated (Owner and Assigned To will always receive notifications).

Attachments: Attach important documents such as signed paperwork and images that are related to this task.

Other Info: The recurrence feature allows you to create automated recurring tasks.
