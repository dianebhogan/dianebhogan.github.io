Sales Workflow

Learn how to operate the four primary components in the ClientSpace Sales Workflow. We will take a new company from initial entry and activity management all the way through the quoting, underwriting, and implementation process. We’ll review the capabilities for configuring persuasive sales outputs, using the DocuSign integration to keep the sales process paperless. Lastly, we’ll review some of the most effective analytical reports and dashboards to drive the sales process.
