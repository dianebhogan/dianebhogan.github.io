Changing your password

You can change your password from the ClientSpace login screen and My Profile after you are logged in.

From the login screen

Changing your password from the login screen sends an email to your external email mailbox, such as Gmail or Outlook. For to send the email, you must have Enable Email for this account enabled in your user profile. Enable Email for this account is how you can receive notifications in your external email mailbox. If this option is not enabled in your user profile, then resetting your password from the login screen will not work. You may need to contact your administrator for assistance.

 

To change your password from the login screen:

  1. Click Forgotten Username or Password? (see the previous image).
    Forgot Password and Forgot Username appear.

  1. To change your password, in Enter your username, type your username, and click Request Password Change.
  2. To request your username, in Enter your email address, type your email address (for example, for Gmail or Outlook), and click Send my Username to me.
  3. Go to your email mailbox, open the email, and follow the instructions.

From My Profile

After you successfully login to ClientSpace, you can change your password from your user profile.

To change your password from My Profile:

  1. Go to My Profile.
    The User Details form opens.
  2. In the Action Center, click (Change Password).


The Change Password dialog box opens.
  1. Complete the fields:
Current Password Enter your current password.
New Password Enter your new password. Follow your organization's requirements for minimum length and complexity.
Confirm Password Enter your new password again.
  1. Click Ok.
  2. Click Save.
