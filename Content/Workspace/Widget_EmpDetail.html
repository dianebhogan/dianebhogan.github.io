Widget: Employee detail info

The Employee Detail Info widget is on the Workers' Comp Claim and other Employee related forms. This widget provides insight into the details of the Employee / Employment records without the need to expand the record.

Widget Definition

Employee Data

Employee name (EmployeeID) (Status).

Job Title

Current Employee Employment Job Title (Workers Comp Code). Click (Jump) to open the Employment record.

Employee Location

Current Employee Employment Location. Click (Jump) to open the Location record.

Last Hire Date

From the Employment Record.
Pay Frequency From the Employment Record.
Last Payroll Date

Most recent Pay Date from Employee Voucher records. Click (Jump) to open the Employee Voucher record.
