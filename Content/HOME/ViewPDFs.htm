Viewing Help in PDF format

By default, your browser might open PDF files using a built-in reader. You may notice fewer features versus opening in Adobe Acrobat.

To view PDFs in Adobe Acrobat:

After you select a guide, Open in Acrobat appears on the right.

Click Open in Acrobat to view the guide using Adobe Acrobat.
